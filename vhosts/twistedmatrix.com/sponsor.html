What do you Mean, 'Supported'?

The Divmod corporation employs several members of the Twisted team, contributes both features and fixes to the Twisted core on a regular basis, and participates in the larger Python community by presenting papers and releasing many open-source projects, both standalone and designed to integrate with Twisted.

A notable contribution is the IMAP4 support, whose initial development was entirely financed by Divmod.
